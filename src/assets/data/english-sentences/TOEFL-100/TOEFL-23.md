## Sentence

<h1>In the absence of a tradition of classical stone-carving and building, the desire to develop Roman amenities would have been difficult to fulfill.</h1>

## Translation

<h2>由于缺乏古典的石材雕刻和建筑的传统，开发罗马风格的便利设施的心愿原本是很难实现的。</h2>


## Vocabulary   

1. **classical**   
a. 经典的，古典的；    

2. **amenity**     
n. [pl.] 生活福利设施，便利设施；     

3. **fulfill**     
vt. 实现，履行；
